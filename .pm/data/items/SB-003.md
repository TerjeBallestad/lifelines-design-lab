## Motivation

Failure should often pay more knowledge than success. A closed bedroom door is a paragraph, not just punishment.

## Scope

- Add a lightweight persistent case-file store.
- Failed/strained attempts add behavior-sentence knowledge based on outcome, carried weakness, room friction, supports, and approach.
- Success can add smaller consolidation entries.
- Render a case-file panel separate from immediate scene evidence.
- Deduplicate repeated entries.

## Acceptance

- Negative/strained outcomes add richer case-file entries than clean success.
- Entries are behavior sentences, not numeric deltas.
- Repeated attempts do not spam duplicates.
