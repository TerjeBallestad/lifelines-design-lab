## Motivation

Playtest 2026-06-11: grayed-out questions show an orange ring but no cue for what is missing or what to do next. Player could not tell a question was gated on more facts.

## Scope

Surface the gating condition on locked open questions, e.g. "trenger N flere fakta" inline, or highlight which fact unlocks it.

## Acceptance

- A locked question states why it is locked and what unlocks it.
- Player can act without trial-and-error clicking.
