## Motivation

The vulnerability-topology spike's strongest question is not "which support is best?" It is: which weakness are we willing to carry today?

The lab currently shows carried weaknesses, but mostly as enum-ish badges. The player needs a readable caseworker decision: two supports improve the posture, but leave a specific failure mode exposed.

## Scope

- Add authored behavior-facing descriptions for each pressure/weakness.
- Upgrade the support-pair panel into a small “what the plan leaves exposed” card.
- Show readable label, behavior sentence, why this pair is vulnerable there, and one room sign to watch for.
- Keep the two-support constraint.

## Acceptance

- Selecting any support pair shows at least one readable carried-weakness sentence.
- No two-support pair displays as fully safe.
- A test verifies every possible two-support pair has at least one carried weakness.
