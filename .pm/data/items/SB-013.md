## Motivation

The clearest gameplay compression so far is:

> Apartment = Citizen Sleeper clocks. Desk = Roottrees board.

The lab should make this visible as the organizing structure rather than a pile of separate panels.

## Scope

Refactor/prototype the lab navigation around two surfaces:

1. **Apartment clocks** — concrete life-practice arcs with dice and paired complications.
2. **Casework desk** — evidence documents, highlighted phrases, claim board, and justified vedtak.

The phone slice should be the first complete loop from apartment clock → evidence → desk claim → next tiltak.

## Acceptance

- The lab presents the current slice as two linked modes/surfaces: Apartment and Desk.
- Apartment mode centers clocks and dice-backed attempts.
- Desk mode centers evidence and claim-mapping.
- A completed attempt produces evidence that appears in the desk.
- A desk claim/vedtak changes the next apartment options.
- The loop can be explained in one sentence inside the UI or README.

## Anti-goals

- No broad dashboard that shows everything at once.
- No full content editor.
- No generic architecture diagram without playable loop proof.
