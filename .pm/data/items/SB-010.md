## Motivation

The Case Desk should follow the Roottrees structure more than a quest log: collect messy evidence, highlight phrases, search documents, connect people/objects/events, and form a casework map. The desk should not diagnose Elling; it should let the player prove enough of a pattern to justify a vedtak.

## Scope

Prototype a small desk surface fed by the phone/telescope slices:

- evidence documents: Frank report, phone bill, bark transcript, object/routine note;
- phrase highlighting / lifted evidence chips;
- a board of claims, unknowns, contradictions, and open questions;
- a “justified vedtak” output when enough evidence supports a next tiltak.

Initial content should be hardcoded. The system shape should be scalable.

## Acceptance

- Player can highlight or select phrases from at least two evidence documents.
- Selected evidence becomes reusable chips/claims on a board.
- The board distinguishes confirmed evidence, hypothesis, contradiction, and open question.
- At least one next tiltak is unlocked/justified by a pattern of evidence, not by a raw progress number.
- The desk copy reads like casework: cautious, specific, and Norwegian-social-realist.

## Anti-goals

- No omniscient diagnosis panel.
- No generic quest log / objective checklist.
- No “case solved” binary; the output is a plausible next vedtak.
