# SB-004/SB-005 generator brief

Sprint: Phone training as staged Citizen Sleeper clock with complication clock

## Implementation target

Replace generic phone practice clocks with a staged Citizen Sleeper-style ladder:

1. Frank says "ring ring" beside the landline.
2. Elling picks up, blurts, hangs up.
3. Frank calls from his mobile while still present.
4. Frank steps into another room and calls the landline.
5. Elling answers independently.

Add a parallel complication clock:

1. Phone becomes interesting.
2. Private call window opens.
3. Sex-line temptation appears.
4. Phone bill lands on the table.

## Generator stance

Keep the implementation small. Make the clock readable and test-protected before touching the desk.
